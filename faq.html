Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

Orders & Shipping

Standard delivery typically takes 7-14 business days within Pakistan. Express delivery options are available for 3-5 business days. Delivery times may vary based on your location and product availability. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on orders over Rs. 50,000 within major cities. For orders below this amount, standard shipping charges apply. Check our shipping policy at checkout for detailed rates.
Absolutely! Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Custom-made or personalized items are not eligible for returns. Contact our customer service team to initiate a return.
Once we receive your returned item, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method. Please allow additional time for your bank to process the refund.
We're sorry if your item arrives damaged. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.
